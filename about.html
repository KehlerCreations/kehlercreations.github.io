ABOUT ME

I am Elliot Kehler.
Independet game developer, writer, and music producer. My primary focus lies today on continued support for A-Star Theft.

As a generalist creative individual, I have found that my specialty lies in figuring out how discrete components intuitively come together in a larger whole.

MY HISTORY

My first tip-toeing into game development started in early 2014, when I discovered YoYoGames' GameMaker 8.1, with which I promply began the creation of the beautiful masterpiece once known as Bob vs Bert. I hope to someday rediscover pictures of this eighth wonder of the world.

After a few months of experimentation and making crude adaptations of other media such as Grand Theft Auto V, I started to build a "faithful" recreation of my favorite game at the time - Payday 2.
