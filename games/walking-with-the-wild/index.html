WISHLIST ON STEAM

Nature so serene… yet so violent.

In this adventure-thriller, you are cast into a mystical world with nothing but your mind and body. To survive, you must leverage your five powerful senses and overcome the horrors that surround you. Can you make it home, or will the journey be too draining?

SENSE ABILITIES

Walking With The Wild is a 3D open-world adventure-thriller where you are cast into a mystical, untamed land with nothing but your mind and body. To survive, you must rely on your five enhanced senses - sight, sound, smell, touch, and intuition - each revealing hidden information about the world around you.

WONDERFUL NATURE

Explore dense forests, murky swamps, hot and cold, with wonder and danger. Creatures roam the land with their own behaviors, territories, and rivalries. Some stalk you. Some flee. Some can be lured into fighting each other — if you understand how to read the environment.

NO COMBAT

You are not a warrior. You are not armed. Survival depends on awareness, stealth, and your ability to interpret the world’s signals before it’s too late. Will you find your way home, or will the wild consume you?
