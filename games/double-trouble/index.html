CO-COMPETETIVE CHAOS

Rush in Guns blazing in a coup! In this competitive mafia game for two players, plan the heist or do your damndest to stop it. Who wins - you, or your friend?

Double Trouble At Bank Noir is a game I developed together with eight other students at the University of Skövde in 2022. There are no plans for future development, but it's fun to keep it up here.
