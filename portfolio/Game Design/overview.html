Level design for Two Dollar Suits

The level design for Two Dollar Suits requires intricate and careful planning.

Level design is both about good interaction layout, and theme-appropriate decoration.

I designed a large casino heist and many others using this sketch format. On this image, you can see NPC placement, security items, AI interactions and more.

Here I showcase one of many levels in the form of a one minute speedrun. The level requires interaction with many different mechanics, which are positioned deliberately throughout the map to encourage exploration, and are balanced for both stealthy and violent tactics.

Weapons system for Two Dollar Suits

Two Dollar Suits features a complex weapons and equipment system designed around the idea of credits. Players have a certain amount of credits available, and different tools require different amounts to use. The game encourages experimentation, as credits are returned once an item is unequipped.
